Jack the Lad

John Norman Chatfield

Jack is a bouncing lad whose enthusiasm for life is enough to drive his parents and brother to distraction. He has a keen sense of style and likes to look like a cool dude. He is fond of computer games, football, singing, yelling, and throwing things. He talks a great deal, and has very firm views about many things, which he is not reluctant to share with others. He has an immensely loud voice which he uses to good effect most mornings.

Jack the Jedi - 2001

A story Jack wrote in a letter to his Granny (dictated to Dad):

"Thank you for the new letter. There was a book and a piece of Lego in it! I thought the card with a picture of kittens playing with some paper was fine. And then they was running about and scared a bird away. And then the bird flied away and went on a wall. And then someone saved her and then a wolf killed the kittens! A bird killed the wolf! And then a cat killed the bird. And then all the persons died. And then the bird came alive again, and that bird was dead again. And they all went inside and then they played in the bedroom. And then they said 'eee", the kittens. No-one died. No-one killed no-one again. They promised they wouldn't kill anyone ever again. They all went to bed and they slept and closed their eyes. And then that's the end."
